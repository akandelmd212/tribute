Tupac Amaru Shakur

One of the greatest musician

Here is a brief history of Tupac's life:

  • Tapac Shakur a.k.a 2pac and Makaveli was born on June 16, 1971 in New York City, US.
  • He was an American rapper, songwriter and an actor
  • He is widely considered one of the most influential rappers of all time
  • He is considered a symbol of activism against inequality
  • He released his debut album titled "2pacalypse Now" in 1991.
  • He later released other albums titled "Strictly 4 my N.I.G.G.A.Z....(1993), Me Against the World (1995), and the first double-length album in hip-hop history, All Eyez On Me (1996)".
  • He also found success as an actor, with his roles in Juice (1992), Poetic Justice (1993), Above the rim (1994), Bullet (1996), Gridlock'd (1997) and Gang Related (1997).
  • He signed to Marion "suge" knights label Death Row Records.
  • Sept 7, 1996, He was shot four times by unidentified assailant in a drive-by shooting in Las Vegas, Nevada, US.
  • Sept. 13, 1996, six days later, he died.
